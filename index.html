About Me

Hello! My name is Kai Curtis and I'm a 25 year old male. I was born in Kansas, only to live there for 3 months before moving to a suburb of Washington D.C. until i was 3. After that we, being a military family, moved to Germany. we spent two years there before my mother, my sisters, and I came back to the States. We moved into a quiet town called Lynchburg in VA. I lived in that town from 1999-2016. In those 16 years I managed to pick up an absolute obsession for skateboarding which has since developed into my lifestyle. As time carried on skateboarding and delivering pizzas didnt seem like a suitable lifestyle so i came to colorado to persue the outdoors and a manufacturing degree. My two years spent getting my associates were a blast. I landed a raft guiding job based in Morrison, the water being clear creek. This company i worked for, Geo tours, not only gave me one of the best job opportunities of my life but also introduced me to my current girlfriend, who is spectacular. You reallly shouldnt need to know anymore.
